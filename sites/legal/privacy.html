Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 30, 2025

1. Introduction

At 1135 Tools, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website.

2. Data Collection

Most of our tools, including the Markdown to PDF converter, are client-side only. This means your data (such as the content you type or files you convert) never leaves your browser and is not stored on our servers.

We may collect minimal technical data such as:

  • IP address (for security and rate limiting).
  • Browser type and version.
  • Page usage statistics (to improve our tools).

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party CDNs (like Cloudflare, jsDelivr) to provide static assets. These services may collect server logs in accordance with their own privacy policies.

If you use donation links (Buy Me a Coffee, Ko-fi, Stripe), those services will handle your payment information directly according to their privacy policies.

4. Cookies

We use local storage only to remember your preferences, such as theme choice (dark/light) and language settings. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data we may handle. Since most processing is done locally on your device, your sensitive data is inherently protected from server-side breaches.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@1135.github.io
